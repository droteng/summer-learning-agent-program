# Curriculum Plan

## Grade 6 Core Summer Camp

Length: 8 weeks

Daily session target: 45-60 minutes

Daily flow:

1. Warm-up puzzle.
2. Main lesson.
3. Practice mission.
4. Creative challenge.
5. Reflection.
6. Body check.
7. Reward progress.

## Weekly Themes

### Week 1: Explorer Mode

- Diagnostic quests.
- Math review.
- Writing baseline.
- Coding intro.
- World map and timeline orientation.

### Week 2: Numbers in the Real World

- Ratios, fractions, decimals, and charts.
- Budget examples.
- Sports and game statistics.
- Data interpretation.

### Week 3: Story Builders

- Reading comprehension.
- Narrative writing.
- Grammar in context.
- Character, plot, and revision.

### Week 4: Code Your First Tool

- Variables.
- Loops.
- Conditionals.
- Debugging.
- Responsible AI basics.

### Week 5: Earth and Inventions

- Science inquiry.
- Ecosystems or earth systems.
- Engineering design.
- Research writing.

### Week 6: World Affairs for Kids

- Geography.
- Current events.
- Fact vs opinion.
- Media literacy foundations.

### Week 7: History Missions

- Ancient civilizations or U.S. civics foundations.
- Timelines.
- Cause and effect.
- Leadership and citizenship.

### Week 8: Capstone Week

- Student chooses a project.
- Options: game, article, presentation, budget plan, history exhibit, science explainer, or invention pitch.
- Parent showcase.

## Optional Enrichment Tracks

Parents choose 2 tracks for a 3-4 week extension.

### Health & Wellness

- Nutrition, hydration, sleep, exercise, and stress.
- Chronic disease awareness: diabetes, hypertension, asthma, obesity, and heart health.
- Focus on energy, strength, focus, mood, and long-term health.
- No diagnosis or body shaming.

### Christian Leadership

- Character, service, honesty, courage, gratitude, humility, responsibility.
- Bible-based reflection only when parent selects it.
- Weekly service or kindness action.

### Financial Literacy

- Saving, spending, budgeting, needs vs wants, allowance, entrepreneurship.

### Media Literacy

- Fact vs opinion, misinformation, advertising, online safety, AI-generated content.

### Creativity / Design

- Storytelling, visual design, presentations, invention, project polish.

### Study Skills

- Focus, memory, time management, notes, planning, and goal setting.

## Daily Body Check

Every daily plan should include a short body check:

- Did you move your body today?
- Did you play outside with parent permission or supervision?
- Did you drink water today?
- Name one healthy food you ate today.
- What time are you aiming to sleep tonight?
